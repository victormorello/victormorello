Iteration of the Self (2022)

Released on the lyon-based Comic Sans Records, Iteration of the Self is a 8 track solo album exploring growth and change. It draws inspiration from a broad set of music genres, from soothing ambient to harsh cinematic noise.

John Carpenter, l'H​é​ritage Musical (2017)

Original music created for the "John Carpenter, l'H​é​ritage Musical" documentary made by Rockyrama. The film featured interviews from Etienne Jaumet, Rob, The Shoes and Carpenter Brut, where they explained the influence Carpenter had on their music.

Mad Max: Univers Brulant (2016)

Ambient music created for the Univers Brulant TV mini-serie made by Rockyrama. It explores the universe and legacy of the Mad Max saga, and features speakers such as George Miller himself, Guillermo Del Toro, Noam Chomsky and Hideo Kojima.
