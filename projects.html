Sound Design & Music

Selected musical works and audio experiments.

Perceptiō (2023)

Perceptiō is a 90 second film created using a mix of analog photographs, videos shot with iPhone, all taken during my various travels. It explores the evolution of perception and senses through a sonified world.

The film won the Sound Creation prize at La Forêt Electrique's Nanometrage contest.

Tu te souviens de la couleur de ma chambre ? (2023)

Created in collaboration with Marion Ellena for her exhibition held Centre Bellegarde in Toulouse, showcasing work created during the 1+2 Residency.

The piece explores the concepts of memory and neuroplasticity, using fragments of Marion's diaries as sound sources, combined with recordings of my personal archives.

Iteration of the Self (2022)

Released on the lyon-based Comic Sans Records, Iteration of the Self is a 8 track solo album exploring growth and change. It draws inspiration from a broad set of music genres, from soothing ambient to harsh cinematic noise.

John Carpenter, l'H​é​ritage Musical (2017)

Original music created for "John Carpenter, l'H​é​ritage Musical", matching the style and technical constraint of the 80s. The film, made by Rockyrama, featured interviews from Etienne Jaumet, Rob, The Shoes and Carpenter Brut, where they explained the influence Carpenter had on their music.

Mad Max: Univers Brulant (2016)

Ambient music created for the Univers Brulant TV mini-serie made by Rockyrama. It explores the universe and legacy of the Mad Max saga, and features speakers such as George Miller himself, Guillermo Del Toro, Noam Chomsky and Hideo Kojima.
