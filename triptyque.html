Triptyque

Design, Programming

Triptyque is a Max for Live synthesiser aimed at creating special effects.

At its core lie 3 sinusoidal oscillators, a frequency modulation matrix and a randomization system.

The sounds it produces sit somewhere between weird textures, like found in the electronic music from the 50s, and harsh noise mayhem.

Its intentionally dry signal path and wide frequency range (from 0 to 20kHz) make it pair very well with any kind of processing, especially time-based and spectral effects.

Download it here or in the Max for Live Device Library.
